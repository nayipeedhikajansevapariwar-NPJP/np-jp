Humara Parichay (About Us)

Nayi Peedhi Ka Janseva Parivar (NPJP) ek grassroots movement hai jo ground level pe kaam karti hai. Hum woh awaaz hain jo unki baat uthate hain jinhe sunne wala koi nahi. Humara mission hai — sach ko saamne lana aur logon ki madad karna.

Humara Mission

  • Ground level issues ko highlight karna
  • Common public ki awaaz banana
  • Corruption aur injustice ke khilaf awaaz uthana
  • Youth ko social work mein involve karna

Humara Vision

  • Ek aisi society banana jahan sab ki sunn ho
  • Youth-led social change movement
  • Transparency aur accountability
  • Har citizen ko empower karna

Humara Kaam (Our Work)

NPJP ke through hum kai tarike se community ki seva karte hain

Field Reporting

Ground level se news aur issues ko document karke saamne laana. Real stories, real impact.

Public Help

Zarooratmand logon ki direct madad karna — medical, legal ya kisi bhi emergency mein.

Awareness Campaigns

Important social issues pe awareness failana — education, health, rights aur responsibilities.

Support Work

Victims aur affected families ko emotional aur practical support dena. Saath khade rehna.
